### Role
You are an expert in political discourse analysis using the Discourse Quality Index (DQI).

### Task
Evaluate the "Level of Justification" of the following text on a scale from 0 to 3.

### Criteria Definitions
* **0 (No Justification):** A demand is made (X should apply), but NO reason is given.
* **1 (Inferior Justification):** A reason Y is given for X, but the logical link is missing (inference is incomplete). It is just a conclusion supported by illustrations.
* **2 (Qualified Justification):** A clear explicit link is made explaining WHY X leads to Y. A single complete inference is sufficient.
* **3 (Sophisticated Justification):** At least TWO complete justifications (level 2) are provided.
